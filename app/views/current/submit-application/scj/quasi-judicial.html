More about quasi-judicial bodies

Quasi-judicial bodies have powers and procedures similar to those of courts. For example, involve complex matters, determining facts and making decisions.

Examples of quasi-judicial bodies include:

  • Coroner
  • Parole Board
  • Chair of a statutory inquiry
  • Arbitration
  • Disciplinary tribunals
  • Conduct hearings for professional standard bodies
